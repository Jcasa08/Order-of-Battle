The World of Ashenfell

Geography

Ashenfell is a continent roughly the size of medieval Europe, bordered by the Endless Ocean to the west, the Bloodstone Mountains to the north, the Shattered Isles to the south, and what is known as the Sylvaan Tangle to the east. The name itself is grim prophecy, ash from countless burnings, and the fell promise of more death to come.
